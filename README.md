This repository contains our machine learning project focused on analyzing and predicting student depression, where we built a model to identify potential mental health risks. Our goal is to understand how various academic and lifestyle factors, such as study habits, academic pressure, and sleep patterns, impact a student's mental well-being. To achieve this, we processed a dataset of student records and trained classification algorithms to accurately detect early signs of depression. Ultimately, this creates a helpful, data-driven tool that can assist in spotting warning signs, providing a proactive approach to supporting student mental health and overall well-being.
